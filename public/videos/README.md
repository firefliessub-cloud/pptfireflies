# Video Assets

## Hero Section Background Video

Place your hero section background video file(s) in this directory:

- **Recommended:** `hero-background.mp4` (MP4 format for best compatibility)
- **Optional:** `hero-background.webm` (WebM format for better compression)

### Video Requirements:
- Format: MP4 (H.264 codec) or WebM
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD) or higher
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9 recommended
- File Size: Optimize for web (aim for 5-10MB or less)
- Duration: Loop-friendly (5-30 seconds recommended)

### Video Tips:
- Keep the video subtle and not too distracting
- Darker videos work better with text overlays
- Consider adding a slight blur or desaturation in post-production
- The video will loop automatically and be muted

If no video is provided, the component will fall back to a gradient background.
